The Chrystal Game!

How To Play The Game:

  1. You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from your until you click on it.
  5. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
